Journey to Västerås ESOW

Reseberättelse till Västerås

Planning

The route to Västerås was planned as a straight line with minor correction to avoid R-area. On the way back, we asked to pass through R-area and received permission to do so at 3000ft or below. Sweden Control felt less busy on the way back, probably because it was in the evening on a Friday.

Outcome

Journey to Västerås (ESOW)

The journey to Västerås was at around 3000ft and it felt very smooth. However, once we approach Västerås, ATC informed us about skydivers, but fortunately they were west of Västerås. There was another plane that was in the traffic circuit so we were hovering at around 2000ft above the airport and landed after that the plane before us did the touch and go and left.

Learnings

  1. Sweden Control can give you clearance in Stockholm Approach airspace.
  1. ATC can grant permission through R-area. Be sure to ask.
  1. It was impossible to see the direction of the wind. We fell back on METAR and other pilots who let us know that they landed on 19.

Journey performed in September 2024. Last updated 22nd of September 2024.
